# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want the R-Searcher community to be welcoming, respectful, and useful for people with different backgrounds, skill levels, and viewpoints.

Participants in this project are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still giving honest feedback.
- Focus on the problem, not the person.
- Share context clearly so others can reproduce issues and help effectively.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, intimidation, or deliberately disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sexualized language or unwelcome sexual attention.

## Scope

This policy applies in project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community channels tied to this repository.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, contact the maintainers privately through the contact path listed in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). If the concern is not security-related, say that in your message and it will still be routed privately.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct. Maintainers may also temporarily or permanently restrict participation when needed to protect the community.
